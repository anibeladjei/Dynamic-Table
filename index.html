Customer Data Management Table

This table allows users to easily store and manage customer information by dynamically adding rows. Fields like customer name, phone number, location, email address, order ID, and other relevant details can be entered and customized as needed. The table adapts to any type of customer data, giving users the flexibility to expand it with new fields when necessary. Any business or adult visiting the site can use this tool to manage their customer information.

Use Case:

Imagine a small business that wants to keep track of its customer details. The business owner can create a table with fields like customer name, phone number, location, and order ID. As customers place new orders, the owner can add a new row with their details and continue expanding the table with more fields like “customer notes” or “last contacted” as needed.

Practice with these Features:

  • Phone Number
  • Location
  • Email Address

You can try adding new rows, customizing these fields, and exploring how to manage your data.
